Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

How do I update my profile information?

Go to your Profile page by clicking on your profile picture and selecting "Profile". From there, you can edit your information and upload a new profile photo.

How do I change my notification settings?

Visit the Settings page and scroll to the Notifications section. There you can customize your email, push, and SMS notification preferences.
